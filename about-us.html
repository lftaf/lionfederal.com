About Us

Lion Federal, positioned at the forefront of Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI), Machine Learning (ML), and Data Science, is a pioneering information technology consulting firm headquartered near the nation's capital. Specializing in custom software development, data science, cloud support services, and facilities design engineering, Lion Federal delivers transformative solutions that significantly enhance efficiency for both the U.S. government and commercial sectors. Through the adoption of an Agile methodology, characterized by daily stand-up SCRUM meetings, the firm ensures the anticipation of customer needs, enabling the delivery of superior solutions swiftly and effectively.

As a certified 8(a) minority-owned Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB) by the SBA, Lion Federal enjoys preferred status for small business contracts with the U.S. government, underscoring its commitment to excellence and innovation in the field. This certification empowers Lion Federal to act as a “sole source” contractor for projects up to $4.5M, offering a streamlined and expedited contracting process that benefits its clients by providing cutting-edge, efficient solutions in shorter time frames.

Our Mission

At Lion Federal our mission is to be a top-tier information technology consulting firm capable of building strategic partnerships in both the government and commercial spaces. We believe that success naturally comes through integrity, work ethic, and commitment to our client’s goals.

Culture and Values

Quality Policy

This will be accomplished through continually improving our services and internal processes and fulfilling our Quality Objectives as defined in our Quality System.

In the Community

Lion Federal believes in supporting the local community. To this end, it is committed to partnering with FACETS (http://www.facetscares.org), a local organization that helps parents, children, and individuals who suffer the effects of poverty in Fairfax County. Lion Federal Principal, Mani Qazi, currently serves on its Board of Directors.

Lion Federal also supports the Air Force Association (https://www.afa.org/), an organization dedicated to advocating for aerospace STEM education and air force veterans.
