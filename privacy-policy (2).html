Privacy Policy for Lion Federal

At Lion Federal, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our employees, clients, and partners. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information, including information collected through SMS messaging services.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Names, contact details (phone numbers, email addresses), and business information provided by employees, clients, and partners
  • Opt-in consent for SMS communication through service agreements or online forms

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Sending project-related updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Responding to inquiries and providing assistance as requested
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and contractual obligations

3. Data Sharing

Lion Federal does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only under the following circumstances:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To government agencies in compliance with federal regulations, policies, and contracts
  • With service providers and partners strictly for operational support (e.g., SMS service providers), ensuring they adhere to privacy and security requirements

4. Opt-In and Consent

We collect SMS opt-in consent through service agreements or forms where phone numbers are provided. By opting in, users agree to receive SMS messages related to Lion Federal operations and projects.

5. Opt-Out and Contact

Users can opt out of SMS communication at any time by replying with the keyword STOP to any message. For further assistance, users may reply with the keyword HELP or contact us via email at info@lionfederal.com.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel and protected by two-step authentication.

7. Changes to this Policy

Lion Federal reserves the right to update this policy to comply with legal and operational changes. Any updates will be posted on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@lionfederal.com
  • Website: https://lionfederal.com
