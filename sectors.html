Sectors

Government

  • SBA 8(a)
  • GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS)

  • SBA 8(a)

Commercial

Lion Federal provides end-to-end solutions for short- and long-term projects for multiple commercial customers. Areas of focus include financial, automotive, and energy industries.
